Isabela and San Cristobal

We offer single day and overnight stays on both of the Galapagos' other two main islands which have plenty to offer. Come with us to learn about the Wall of Tears and snorkel around the lava tunnels of Isabela. See the antics of its namesake at Flamingo Lake, visit a Flightless Cormorant colony and so much more! San Cristobal boasts stunning highlands, gorgeous beaches and of course, lots of animals to see. Let us take you to Cerro Tijeretas where you'll meet Darwins statue and head to Baquerizo beach below, or up to see el Junco lagoon. Get in touch to find out more about tours to these and other amazing places.

Scuba Diving

With some of the most abundant and unique sea life in the world, you would be mad not to pull on a wetsuit and come diving with us! Santa Cruz has some amazing dive sites, Gordon Rocks being one of the most popular due to the variety of aquatic creatures. But be warned, it can be a tricky dive site with strong, swirling currents often compared to being in a washing machine! If you're experience and brave enough, then the sights are worth it; with a great chance to see turtles, a variety of fish, rays, Galapagos, White and Black Tip sharks along with the famous Hammerheads. Get in touch with us to find out more about Gordon Rocks and other dive sites we visit.
