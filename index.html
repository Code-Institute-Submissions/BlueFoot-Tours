Who are we?

About Us BlueFoot Tours is a locally owned and operated guide company that has beentaking visitors to see the best the Galapagos Islands have to offer for over 15 years. We offer small group, personalised tours both on land and sea.Whether you want to hang out with the famous Giant Tortoises or scuba dive beneath the waves in search of Hammerhead sharks and sea lions, we will get you there! The Islands are home to many unique species such as the Galapagos Giant Tortoise, the Blue Footed Boobie and the Marine Iguana. You will encounter    The Owners

They understand what it's like travelling on a budget and started BlueFoot Tours with that in mind.  A visit to The Galapagos Islands is for many, a once in a lifetime experience; Get in contact with us and let us help you achieve your dream in the most unique place on Earth.
